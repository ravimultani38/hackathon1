About CUNY Connect

CUNY Connect is your one-stop platform for staying informed about all the important updates and notifications across the City University of New York (CUNY) system. Whether it's food pantry information, library updates, gym notifications, or fire drill alerts, we've got you covered.

Built for students, faculty, and staff, CUNY Connect ensures that you never miss out on critical announcements from your campus. Our mission is to enhance communication within the CUNY community by providing a centralized and easy-to-use platform for all your informational needs.

This project was developed as part of the CTP Hackathon, where our goal was to create a tool that could make a difference in the daily lives of CUNY students. We believe that with better information, you can make better decisions.

Key Features:

  • Customizable notifications to match your preferences.
  • Easy access to essential campus resources and services.
  • Comming soon: Real-time updates from all CUNY campuses.

We are committed to continuously improving CUNY Connect and welcome your feedback. Let's work together to make the CUNY experience better for everyone.
